Stop Waiting for EMS Specialists.

Streamline Building Energy Data Faster. Pre-configured Energy Management Systems (EMS) can be easily commissioned by your on-site electrician, reducing project delays and specialist costs.

The problem

Complicated and Expensive

Putting in place energy monitoring systems on-site can be a complex and time-consuming process. Using existing methods requires specialist programmers, delays your project while waiting for them, and creates budget and timeline uncertainty.

Our Solution: The "Flat-Pack" Approach to On-Site EMS

Our "Ready-to-Assemble" kit provides everything you need—both the pre-configured hardware and the simple software—to get your energy monitoring online. By simplifying custom programming, we can now deliver results in three easy steps:

Simple Steps

Simple Configuration

We use a simple, familiar spreadsheet to define all project meters and data points. No complex coding is required.

Pre-configured Hardware

Our system uses that spreadsheet to automatically generate the full configuration for our "monitoring-in-a-box" hardware module.

Fast Deployment

Your on-site electrician can deploy this complete kit in minutes, bringing your energy monitoring online without hiring specialists.
