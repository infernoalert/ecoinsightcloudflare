Understanding the Future of Electrical Design

An independent research project focused on the workflow challenges of NCC 2022

Our Mission

The National Construction Code is evolving rapidly, bringing new challenges and opportunities for electrical engineers across Australia. Our mission is to deeply understand the day-to-day workflow of Australia's electrical engineers and identify how NCC 2022 impacts their design processes, decision-making, and project delivery.

Our Approach: Genchi Genbutsu

Direct Observation
